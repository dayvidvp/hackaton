When the customer or transport company arrives to pick up the goods, the warehouse operator needs to

**Load to truck**.

After scanning the barcode on the **Outbound load list**, he can scan the different license plates mentioned on the list. The labels are on the goods. When the last license plate is scanned, the load is ready to be confirmed by the administration.

If the warehouse operator doesn\'t scan the license plates before loading the goods physically, it is possible to manually recreate this action in the Warehouse management app.

Go to: **Outbound** \> **Load in truck**.

![](media/image2.png){width="0.8645833333333334in" height="0.7083333333333334in"}Click on the icon **Load list**.

Select the load from the list by clicking on it.

![](media/image3.png){width="6.268055555555556in" height="2.95625in"}

Now click on the icon **Load license plates list.** ![](media/image4.png){width="0.9584667541557306in" height="0.625087489063867in"}

![](media/image5.png){width="6.268055555555556in" height="0.9604166666666667in"}

Select the license plate(s) with No and confirm loading them. When all the license plates are loaded (Yes), the loading is finished, and the outbound shipment can be confirmed (either manually or automatically).
